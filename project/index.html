Final Year Project: Study of Local Descriptors for Robust Visual Place Recognition
Completed
This project addresses the efficiency bottleneck in Visual Place Recognition (VPR) by improving local descriptor reranking performance. We propose enhancements to the Attentive Patch algorithm through two key innovations: an improved anchor selection strategy and an adaptive thresholding mechanism. Our methods achieved better accuracy while maintaining computational efficiency, particularly in challenging scenarios with significant environmental variations.
