The Gaussian Splatting Toolkit is an innovative open-source framework that focuses on new view synthesis using Gaussian splatting techniques. Built on top of nerfstudio and specifically designed for robotics research, this toolkit provides a comprehensive suite of tools for 3D scene reconstruction and rendering.

## Key Features

- Camera calibration and depth alignment capabilities
- Advanced training pipeline for Gaussian splatting
- Depth supervision support (both sensor and mono depth estimation)
- State-of-the-art mesh extraction functionality
- Robust visualization and rendering tools

The toolkit has gained recognition in the field, being integrated into the MANI Foundation Model, demonstrating its practical value in advanced 3D reconstruction applications.

## Impact

Developed with both researchers and practitioners in mind, this toolkit simplifies the complex process of 3D scene reconstruction while maintaining high-quality results. It's particularly valuable for robotics applications where accurate scene understanding and reconstruction are crucial.
